# Potions & Effects

## Brewing
- Brewing stand: blaze rod + 3 cobblestone
- Fuel: blaze powder (20 brews per powder)
- Base: water bottle (glass bottle in water)
- Step 1: water bottle + nether wart = awkward potion (base for most)
- Step 2: awkward potion + ingredient = effect potion
- Modifier: + redstone = longer duration, + glowstone = stronger (level II)
- Splash: + gunpowder = throwable splash potion
- Lingering: + dragon breath = area effect (skip, no dragon)
- Brew time: 20 sec per step

## Potion Effects

### Positive
- Speed I/II: +20%/+40% movement speed (3min/1:30)
- Strength I/II: +3/+6 melee dmg (3min/1:30)
- Regeneration I/II: 1HP per 2.5sec/1.25sec (45sec/22sec)
- Healing I/II: instant +4/+8 HP
- Fire Resistance: immune to fire/lava (3min)
- Night Vision: see in dark (3min)
- Water Breathing: breathe underwater (3min)
- Invisibility: invisible to mobs at distance, no nametag (3min)
- Jump Boost I/II: +50%/+100% jump height (3min/1:30)
- Slow Falling: float down, no fall dmg (1:30)
- Luck: better loot from fishing/chests (5min)

### Negative
- Slowness I/IV: -15%/-60% speed (1:30/20sec)
- Weakness: -4 melee dmg (1:30)
- Poison I/II: 1HP per 2.5sec/1.25sec, can't kill (45sec/22sec)
- Harming I/II: instant -6/-12 HP
- Blindness: fog at 3 blocks distance
- Hunger: food depletes faster
- Mining Fatigue I-III: mining speed reduced
- Wither: like poison but CAN kill, black hearts (from wither skeleton)
- Bad Omen: triggers raid on village entry
- Nausea: warped screen (from pufferfish)

## Key Ingredients
- Nether wart: base for most potions (need to obtain somehow without Nether)
- Spider eye: poison
- Fermented spider eye: inverts effect (healing->harming, speed->slowness)
- Blaze powder: strength
- Magma cream: fire resistance
- Ghast tear: regeneration
- Sugar: speed
- Rabbit foot: jump boost (leaping)
- Pufferfish: water breathing
- Golden carrot: night vision
- Phantom membrane: slow falling
- Glistering melon: healing
- Turtle shell: water breathing (helmet)

## NOTE: Nether Wart Sourcing
- In MC: nether wart only in Nether fortresses
- Sky Craft solution: nether wart found in loot chests on volcanic/lava islands, or grows on soul sand near lava pools on special islands
