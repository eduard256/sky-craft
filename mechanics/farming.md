# Farming & Agriculture

## Farmland
- Created by using hoe on dirt/grass block
- Hydrated: water within 4 blocks, dark texture, crops grow faster
- Dry farmland: no water nearby, reverts to dirt if not planted within a while
- Trampled (reverts to dirt): jumping/falling on it, mobs walking on it

## Wheat
- Plant: seeds on farmland (seeds from breaking tall grass, ~10% drop)
- Growth: 8 stages, ~10-30 min (random tick based)
- Harvest: drops 1 wheat + 0-3 seeds when fully grown
- Use: bread (3 wheat), cake, breed cows/sheep

## Carrots
- Plant: carrot on farmland
- Growth: 8 stages, same as wheat
- Harvest: 1-4 carrots
- Use: food (3 hunger), breed pigs, golden carrot crafting

## Potatoes
- Plant: potato on farmland
- Growth: 8 stages, same as wheat
- Harvest: 1-4 potatoes + 2% chance poisonous potato
- Use: baked potato (furnace), breed pigs

## Beetroot
- Plant: beetroot seeds on farmland
- Growth: 4 stages
- Harvest: 1 beetroot + 0-3 seeds
- Use: beetroot soup (6 hunger), red dye

## Melon
- Plant: melon seeds on farmland, grows stem
- Stem grows to full, then spawns melon block on adjacent dirt/farmland
- Break melon block: 3-7 melon slices
- Regrows without replanting

## Pumpkin
- Same as melon: seed -> stem -> pumpkin on adjacent block
- Harvest: pumpkin block (1)
- Use: jack o'lantern, pumpkin pie, snow/iron golem head, carved pumpkin (shears)

## Sugar Cane
- Plant on sand/dirt adjacent to water, no farmland needed
- Grows up to 3 blocks tall
- Break middle = top falls, bottom stays and regrows
- Use: paper (3 sugar cane = 3 paper), sugar

## Cactus
- Plant on sand only, grows up to 3 blocks tall
- Cannot have adjacent blocks (breaks if neighbor placed)
- Deals 1 dmg on contact
- Use: green dye (smelting), trash disposal (items touching cactus destroyed)

## Bamboo
- Plant on dirt/grass/sand, grows very fast (up to 12-16 blocks)
- Use: sticks, scaffolding, bamboo planks

## Cocoa Beans
- Grow on jungle log sides, 3 stages
- Harvest: 1-3 cocoa beans
- Use: brown dye, cookies

## Trees (Saplings)
- Plant sapling on dirt/grass with space above
- Grow randomly (bone meal speeds up)
- Oak: 1 sapling, needs 5+ blocks above
- Birch: 1 sapling, needs 6+ blocks
- Spruce: 1 or 4 (2x2 for giant), needs 7+ or 15+
- Jungle: 1 or 4 (2x2 for giant), needs 5+ or 12+
- Acacia: 1 sapling, diagonal growth
- Dark oak: must be 4 (2x2), needs 7+
- Drops: logs + leaves, leaves decay -> saplings + apples (oak)

## Bone Meal
- Crafted from bones (skeleton drop), 1 bone = 3 bone meal
- Instantly grows crops 2-5 stages
- Grows sapling to tree (multiple uses, random)
- Grows tall grass and flowers on grass blocks
- Grows seagrass underwater

## Animal Breeding
- Feed 2 adults correct food -> hearts -> baby in 5 sec
- Baby grows up in 20 min (speed up with food)
- Cooldown: 5 min between breeding same animal
- Drops 1-7 XP on breeding
