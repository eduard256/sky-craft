# Passive Mobs

## Cow
- HP: 10, drops: 1-3 leather, 1-3 raw beef, 1-3 XP
- Breedable with wheat, baby grows in 20 min
- Right-click with bucket = milk (clears potion effects)
- Spawns in groups of 2-4 on grass, light 9+

## Pig
- HP: 10, drops: 1-3 raw porkchop, 1-3 XP
- Breedable with carrots/potatoes/beetroot
- Rideable with saddle, controlled with carrot on stick
- Speed when ridden: ~5 blocks/sec

## Sheep
- HP: 8, drops: 1 wool (sheared: 1-3 wool), 1-2 mutton, 1-3 XP
- Breedable with wheat. Shearing with shears gets wool without killing
- Wool regrows after eating grass block (turns to dirt)
- Spawn colors: white 82%, light gray 5%, gray 5%, black 5%, brown 3%
- Dyeable with any of 16 dye colors

## Chicken
- HP: 4, drops: 1-2 feathers, 1 raw chicken, 1-3 XP
- Breedable with seeds (wheat/melon/pumpkin/beetroot seeds)
- Lays egg every 5-10 min, throw egg = 1/8 chance to spawn chick
- Falls slowly (flaps wings), no fall damage
- Baby grows in 20 min

## Horse
- HP: 15-30 (varies), drops: 0-2 leather, 1-3 XP
- Tamed by repeatedly mounting until hearts appear
- Rideable with saddle, speed varies 4.7-14.2 blocks/sec
- Jump strength varies, can equip horse armor
- Breedable with golden apples/carrots

## Donkey
- HP: 15-30, drops: 0-2 leather, 1-3 XP
- Same taming as horse, slower than horse
- Can equip chest (15 inventory slots)
- Breedable with golden apples/carrots

## Wolf
- HP: 8 wild / 20 tamed, drops: 1-3 XP
- Tamed with bones (1/3 chance per bone)
- Tamed wolf attacks what owner attacks or what attacks owner
- Healed by feeding meat, tail angle shows HP
- Breedable with meat when tamed

## Cat (Ocelot)
- HP: 10, drops: 0-2 string, 1-3 XP
- Tamed with raw cod/salmon (must sneak up slowly)
- Creepers and phantoms avoid cats within 6 blocks
- 11 cat variants, breedable with raw fish

## Rabbit
- HP: 3, drops: 0-1 rabbit hide, 0-1 raw rabbit, 1-3 XP
- Breedable with carrots/dandelions
- Killer bunny variant: hostile, rare (disabled by default, we skip this)

## Squid
- HP: 10, drops: 1-3 ink sacs, 1-3 XP
- Spawns in water, any light level
- Swims aimlessly, squirts ink cloud when hit

## Bat
- HP: 6, drops: nothing, 0 XP
- Ambient mob, spawns in dark areas below Y=63
- Hangs upside down on ceilings when idle
- Purely decorative, no interaction

## Villager
- HP: 20, drops: nothing
- Has profession based on workstation block (see villages.md)
- Trades items for emeralds and vice versa
- Levels up through trading: Novice > Apprentice > Journeyman > Expert > Master
- Sleeps in beds at night, runs from hostile mobs
- Breeds when enough beds + food available
- Gossip system: prices change based on player reputation

## Iron Golem
- HP: 100, drops: 3-5 iron ingots, 0-2 poppies
- Spawns naturally in villages (1 per 10 villagers + 21 beds)
- Can be built: 4 iron blocks T-shape + carved pumpkin head
- Attacks hostile mobs, 7-21 dmg, launches targets into air
- Doesn't take fall damage, can't drown

## Snow Golem
- HP: 4, drops: 0-15 snowballs
- Built: 2 snow blocks + carved pumpkin
- Throws snowballs at hostile mobs (0 dmg but knockback)
- Leaves snow trail on ground, takes dmg in warm biomes
- Dies in rain/water

## Parrot
- HP: 6, drops: 1-2 feathers, 1-3 XP
- Tamed with seeds, sits on player shoulder
- Imitates nearby mob sounds
- Killed by feeding cookie (chocolate = poison to parrots)

## Turtle
- HP: 30, drops: 0-2 seagrass, 1-3 XP
- Breedable with seagrass, lays eggs on home beach
- Baby turtle drops scute when growing up
- 5 scutes = turtle shell helmet (water breathing 10 sec)

## Fox
- HP: 20, drops: nothing, 1-3 XP
- Spawns in taiga biomes, nocturnal
- Picks up items in mouth, can be bred with sweet berries
- Baby fox trusts player if bred by player

## Bee
- HP: 10, drops: nothing, 1-3 XP
- Pollinates flowers, returns to nest/hive
- Full hive can be harvested with shears (honeycomb) or bottle (honey)
- Stings attacker once then dies, deals 1 dmg + poison 10 sec
- Smoke from campfire prevents aggro during harvest

## Fish (Cod, Salmon, Tropical, Pufferfish)
- Cod: HP 3, drops raw cod
- Salmon: HP 3, drops raw salmon
- Tropical: HP 3, drops tropical fish (decorative)
- Pufferfish: HP 3, drops pufferfish (poison + water breathing potion ingredient)
- All spawn in water, catchable with bucket
