# Block Types & Properties

## Natural Blocks
- Stone, cobblestone, deepslate, granite, diorite, andesite, tuff, calcite
- Dirt, grass block, coarse dirt, rooted dirt, podzol, mycelium, mud
- Sand, red sand, gravel, clay, sandstone, red sandstone
- Ores: coal, iron, copper, gold, diamond, lapis, emerald, redstone (all deepslate variants)
- Bedrock: NOT used in Sky Craft (void instead)
- Obsidian: water + lava source, minable with diamond pick (9.4s)
- Ice, packed ice, blue ice
- Snow block, snow layer, powder snow
- Magma block: dmg 1/sec standing, not sneaking
- Soul sand: slows movement, nether wart grows on it
- Soul soil: soul fire (blue), campfire variant
- Netherrack: keep for volcanic islands, fire burns forever on top
- Basalt, blackstone: volcanic island building blocks
- Amethyst: geodes inside some islands, grows amethyst clusters

## Wood / Organic
- Log, stripped log, wood, stripped wood (oak, birch, spruce, jungle, acacia, dark oak)
- Planks, slabs, stairs, fences, fence gates, doors, trapdoors, signs, boats (per wood type)
- Leaves (all types): decay when no log within 7 blocks, drop saplings
- Bamboo, bamboo block, bamboo planks
- Mushroom blocks (red, brown), mushroom stem
- Hay bale: 9 wheat, reduces fall dmg by 80%
- Pumpkin, carved pumpkin, jack o'lantern
- Melon block
- Cactus: damages entities, grows on sand

## Crafted / Processed
- Bricks, stone bricks (+ cracked, mossy, chiseled)
- Smooth stone, polished variants (granite, diorite, andesite, deepslate)
- Glass, stained glass (16 colors), glass pane
- Wool (16 colors), carpet (16 colors)
- Terracotta (16 colors), glazed terracotta
- Concrete powder -> concrete (when touches water), 16 colors
- Copper block: oxidizes over time (4 stages), waxed with honeycomb
- Iron block, gold block, diamond block, emerald block, lapis block
- Bookshelf: 3 books + 6 planks, needed for enchanting

## Functional Blocks
- Crafting table, furnace, blast furnace, smoker
- Anvil (3 damage stages)
- Enchanting table
- Brewing stand
- Chest, barrel, ender chest, shulker box
- Bed (16 colors): sleep, set spawn
- Torch, soul torch, lantern, soul lantern
- Campfire, soul campfire: cook 4 items, smoke signal, prevents bee aggro
- Ladder, scaffolding
- Sign, hanging sign: display text
- Lectern: holds book, emits redstone (postpone signal)
- Cauldron: holds water/lava/powder snow
- Composter: converts plant matter to bone meal
- Loom: weave banner patterns
- Cartography table: zoom/clone/lock maps
- Smithing table: upgrade gear (netherite -> skip, but table exists for villager profession)
- Stonecutter: cut stone variants
- Grindstone: remove enchants, repair
- Bell: alert villagers, shows raid progress
- Beacon: needs pyramid of iron/gold/diamond/emerald blocks, gives area buffs (speed/strength/etc)
- Jukebox: plays music discs
- Note block: plays notes when hit (postpone with redstone)
- TNT: see explosions.md
- Spawner: spawns mobs, found in dungeons, can't be obtained

## Decorative
- Flower types: dandelion, poppy, blue orchid, allium, azure bluet, tulips, etc
- Tall grass, fern, tall fern, dead bush
- Vine: climbable on walls
- Lily pad: walkable on water
- Coral blocks + coral fans (warm ocean islands)
- Moss block, moss carpet, dripleaf
- Glow lichen: dim light, grows on surfaces
- Candle: up to 4 per block, different light levels
- Banner: 16 colors, patterns via loom
- Skull/head: mob/player heads, decorative
- Flower pot: holds small plants
- Painting: decorative wall art (random sizes)
- Item frame, glow item frame
