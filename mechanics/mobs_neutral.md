# Neutral Mobs

## Enderman
- See mobs_hostile.md (neutral until provoked)

## Llama
- HP: 15-30, drops: 0-2 leather, 1-3 XP
- Spawns in savanna/mountain biomes
- Tamed by riding repeatedly
- Equip chest: 3/6/9/12/15 slots depending on strength
- Equip carpet for decoration
- Spits at wolves and attackers (1 dmg)
- Forms caravans: lead 1 llama, up to 10 follow

## Polar Bear
- HP: 30, drops: 0-2 raw cod, 1-3 XP
- Spawns in snowy biomes
- Neutral, attacks if baby nearby or if attacked
- Baby polar bear: passive

## Dolphin
- HP: 10, drops: 0-1 raw cod, 1-3 XP
- Spawns in water (ocean-type islands with water)
- Gives speed boost to nearby swimming players
- Leads player to treasure if fed raw cod
- Attacks back if hit, attacks in groups

## Panda
- HP: 20, drops: 1 bamboo, 1-3 XP
- Spawns in jungle biome on bamboo
- 7 personality types: normal, lazy, worried, playful, aggressive, weak, brown
- Aggressive pandas attack if hit
- Breedable with bamboo

## Goat
- HP: 10, drops: nothing, 1-3 XP
- Spawns on mountain islands
- Randomly rams players/mobs (knockback 9 blocks)
- Drops goat horn when ramming solid block (horn = raid sound)
- Jumps high (10 blocks), avoids powder snow
- Screaming goat variant: rams more often

## Piglin (adapted for Sky Craft)
- NOTE: originally Nether mob
- For Sky Craft: could exist on volcanic/gold-rich islands
- HP: 16, drops: gold items
- Barters: give gold ingot, get random item
- Hostile unless player wears gold armor
- Skip or adapt for V1

## Zombified Piglin
- NOTE: originally Nether mob
- HP: 20, drops: rotten flesh, gold nugget, gold ingot (rare)
- Neutral, attacks in groups when one is hit
- For Sky Craft: volcanic islands, near lava
- Fire immune

## Spider (daytime)
- Neutral during day, hostile at night/dark
- See mobs_hostile.md for full details
