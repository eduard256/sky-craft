# Explosions & TNT

## TNT
- Activated by: redstone signal, fire, flaming arrow, explosion, flint&steel
- Fuse time: 4 sec (80 ticks), blinks white
- Becomes entity when activated (affected by gravity, falls)
- Explosion power: 4 (destroys blocks in ~3-4 block radius)
- Drops ~30% of destroyed blocks as items (rest lost)
- Entity dmg: up to 65 HP at point blank, decreases with distance
- Blast resistance: determines if block survives (obsidian, bedrock = survive)
- Water/lava: TNT in water destroys no blocks but still damages entities

## Creeper Explosion
- Power: 3 (slightly less than TNT)
- Charged creeper: power 6 (double)
- Charged creepers make mobs drop their heads (skull item)
- No block drops from creeper explosions (unlike TNT)

## Ghast Fireball
- Power: 1 (small explosion)
- Sets fire on impact
- Can be reflected by hitting it back (any attack)

## Bed Explosion (not applicable -- single dimension only)

## Blast Resistance (common blocks)
- Stone: 6, Cobblestone: 6
- Dirt/grass: 0.5 (very weak)
- Wood planks: 2
- Obsidian: 1200 (basically indestructible by explosions)
- Water: 100 (absorbs blast)
- Iron block: 6
- Diamond block: 6
- Glass: 0.3 (very weak)

## Fire Spread
- Fire ignites flammable blocks: wood, planks, leaves, wool, carpet, fences
- Spread distance: up to 1 block away on surface, 4 blocks down
- Fire burns out after 3-40 sec depending on block
- Eternal fire: on netherrack (keep for lava islands) and soul sand
- Rain extinguishes fire
- Fire tick gamerule controls spread (disable for grief protection)
