# Hostile Mobs

## Zombie
- HP: 20, dmg: 2-4 (easy-hard), drops: 0-2 rotten flesh, rare: iron ingot/carrot/potato
- Burns in sunlight (unless wearing helmet or in water)
- Attacks villagers, can break wooden doors on hard
- Baby zombie: faster, doesn't burn in sun, fits in 1-block gaps
- Can pick up items/armor from ground and equip them
- Zombie villager: can be cured with weakness potion + golden apple

## Skeleton
- HP: 20, dmg: 1-5 (varies by difficulty + range), drops: 0-2 bones, 0-2 arrows
- Burns in sunlight, shoots arrows, strafes around target
- Takes cover in shade/water during day
- Can pick up items, wears armor if found
- Shoots every 2 sec, accuracy depends on difficulty

## Creeper
- HP: 20, drops: 0-2 gunpowder, music disc (if killed by skeleton arrow)
- Silent approach, starts 1.5 sec fuse when within 3 blocks
- Explosion: ~3 block radius, destroys blocks (unless mobGriefing off)
- Charged creeper (struck by lightning): 2x explosion power
- No burn in sunlight, spawns only in dark
- Afraid of cats/ocelots (runs away within 6 blocks)

## Spider
- HP: 16, dmg: 2-3, drops: 0-2 string, 0-1 spider eye, 5 XP
- Hostile at night / dark, neutral in daylight
- Climbs walls, fits in 2-wide 1-tall gap
- Can spawn with potion effects (speed/strength/invisibility/regen) on hard
- Spider jockey: skeleton riding spider (1% spawn chance)

## Cave Spider
- HP: 12, dmg: 2-3 + poison (1.5 sec easy, 15 sec hard)
- Spawns only from spawners in mineshafts (we may skip or adapt)
- Fits in 1x1x0.5 gaps, climbs walls like spider

## Enderman
- HP: 40, dmg: 4-7, drops: 0-1 ender pearl, 5 XP
- Neutral until looked at (crosshair on face) or attacked
- Teleports randomly and to dodge projectiles
- Takes dmg from water/rain
- Can pick up certain blocks (grass, dirt, sand, etc)
- 3 blocks tall, doesn't fit under 2-block ceilings
- NOTE: no End dimension but endermen still spawn in overworld at night (rare)

## Witch
- HP: 26, drops: 0-6 various (glowstone, sugar, redstone, spider eye, glass bottle, stick)
- Throws harmful splash potions: poison, slowness, weakness, harming
- Drinks defensive potions: healing, fire resist, water breathing, speed
- Spawns in dark, also in witch huts (swamp biome)

## Slime
- HP: big=16 med=4 small=1, drops: 0-2 slimeballs (small only)
- Spawns below Y=40 in specific chunks (slime chunks) OR in swamp at night
- Splits on death: big->2-4 med, med->2-4 small, small->dies
- Hops toward player, deals dmg on contact (big: 4, med: 2, small: 0)

## Phantom
- HP: 20, dmg: 2-3, drops: 0-1 phantom membrane, 5 XP
- Spawns in sky when player hasn't slept for 3+ days
- Dives at player from above, burns in sunlight
- Phantom membrane: repairs elytra, brews slow falling potion
- Afraid of cats within 16 blocks

## Drowned
- HP: 20, dmg: 2-4, drops: 0-2 rotten flesh, rare: trident (8.5%), nautilus shell
- Spawns in oceans/rivers/water (we have water on islands)
- Zombie that fell in water converts to drowned after 30 sec
- Can throw tridents at range
- Trident is a key weapon drop (melee + ranged)

## Guardian
- HP: 30, dmg: 6 (laser), drops: 0-2 prismarine shards, 0-1 raw cod, 10 XP
- NOTE: normally spawns in ocean monuments. For Sky Craft: could spawn in water-themed islands
- Laser attack: charges 2 sec then deals dmg, only works in water
- Inflicts Mining Fatigue III on nearby players (elder guardian only)

## Elder Guardian
- HP: 80, drops: prismarine shards, wet sponge, 10 XP
- Boss-like mob, 3 per ocean monument
- Mining Fatigue III curse in 50-block radius
- For Sky Craft: could be mini-boss on rare water islands

## Pillager
- HP: 24, dmg: 3-5, drops: 0-2 arrows, crossbow (rare), 5 XP
- Spawns at pillager outposts, patrols
- Attacks villagers and players, uses crossbow
- Killing captain (banner-wearing) gives Bad Omen effect -> triggers raid

## Vindicator
- HP: 24, dmg: 7-19 (iron axe!), drops: 0-1 emerald, iron axe (rare), 5 XP
- Spawns in raids and woodland mansions
- Charges at player with axe, high melee dmg
- One of the most dangerous common mobs

## Evoker
- HP: 24, drops: 0-1 emerald, 1 totem of undying, 10 XP
- Spawns in raids (wave 5+) and woodland mansions
- Summons vexes (flying ghosts) and fangs (ground attack)
- Totem of undying: prevents death once when held

## Ravager
- HP: 100, dmg: 12, drops: saddle, 20 XP
- Spawns in raids, large beast ridden by pillagers
- Charges and destroys crops/leaves on contact
- Stunned briefly when blocked with shield

## Vex
- HP: 14, dmg: 5-13, drops: nothing, 3 XP
- Summoned by evokers, flies through blocks
- Starts taking dmg after 30-120 sec (limited lifespan)
- Small, fast, hard to hit

## Silverfish
- HP: 8, dmg: 1, drops: nothing, 5 XP
- Hides in infested stone/cobble/brick blocks
- When hit, calls nearby silverfish from infested blocks
- For Sky Craft: can exist in stone-rich islands

## Stray
- HP: 20, drops: 0-2 bones, 0-2 arrows, tipped arrow of slowness (50%)
- Skeleton variant in snowy biomes
- Arrows apply Slowness I for 30 sec

## Husk
- HP: 20, drops: 0-2 rotten flesh, rare items like zombie
- Zombie variant in desert biomes
- Doesn't burn in sun, attacks apply Hunger effect

## Wither Skeleton
- HP: 20, dmg: 5-10, drops: coal, bones, wither skull (2.5%)
- NOTE: normally Nether mob. For Sky Craft: could spawn on volcanic/lava islands as rare mob
- Attacks apply Wither effect (10 sec, dmg over time)
- 2.5 blocks tall, carries stone sword

## Witch (see above, listed once)

## Magma Cube
- HP: big=16 med=4 small=1, drops: 0-1 magma cream (all sizes)
- NOTE: normally Nether mob. For Sky Craft: lava/volcanic islands
- Like slime but fire resistant, bounces higher
- Splits on death like slime

## Blaze
- HP: 20, dmg: 5 (melee) + fireball 5, drops: 0-1 blaze rod, 10 XP
- NOTE: normally Nether mob. For Sky Craft: volcanic islands
- Flies, shoots 3 fireballs in burst, fire resistant
- Blaze rod essential for brewing stands + eyes of ender
- Takes dmg from water/snowballs

## Ghast
- HP: 10, drops: 0-1 ghast tear, 0-2 gunpowder, 5 XP
- NOTE: normally Nether. For Sky Craft: could roam void between islands
- Shoots explosive fireballs, can be reflected by hitting back
- Flies, large (4x4x4 blocks), cries when idle
- Fireballs destroy blocks on impact

## Warden
- HP: 500, dmg: 16-45, drops: sculk catalyst, 5 XP
- Spawns when sculk shrieker activated 3+ times
- Blind, detects by sound + smell (vibrations + sniffing)
- Sonic boom ranged attack bypasses armor, 10 dmg
- NOTE: For Sky Craft: could be deep underground on large islands or skip entirely
