# Sounds & Music

## Sound System
- 3D positional audio: volume/pan based on distance and direction
- Max hearing distance: 16 blocks for most sounds
- Categories: master, music, blocks, hostile, friendly, players, ambient, weather
- Each category has independent volume slider

## Block Sounds
- Each block type has: place, break, step, hit, fall sounds
- Material groups: wood, stone, gravel, grass, metal, glass, wool, sand, snow, coral
- Pitch/volume varies slightly for natural feel (random +/- 10%)

## Entity Sounds
- Mobs: idle, hurt, death, ambient sounds
- Hostile mobs: step sounds help player locate them
- Animals: breed sound (hearts), baby sounds (higher pitch)
- Player: hurt grunt, eat sounds, level up ding

## Environment
- Rain: ambient loop, varies with intensity
- Thunder: loud crack, random timing during thunderstorm
- Underwater: muffled ambient, bubble sounds
- Cave ambience: random eerie sounds in dark areas (mood sounds)
- Wind: for Sky Craft, ambient wind sound when on island edges/void

## Music
- Background music: plays randomly every 10-30 min
- Biome-specific tracks from MC soundtrack
- Music discs: found in dungeon chests, play in jukebox
- We use MC assets so all MC music/sounds available
